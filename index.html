In September 2024, the university of Nigeria Nsukka welcomed the Sui on Canpus team to campus in an explosion and impactful event which brought young people together to create innovative solutions on the Sui Blockchain. One same day, the team Seiko was formed and we have been doing amazing work to ensure our solution adequately addresses the problem.
